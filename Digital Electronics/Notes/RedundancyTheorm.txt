Redundancy Theorm : 
	->Three variable
	->Each variable is repeated twice
	->One variable is complimented
	->Take the complimented variable
	
	->Ex.:	 AB + A'B + BC
		->Ans.: AB + A'C
		
	->Ex.:	 AB + AC + BC'
		->Ans.: AC + BC'
		
	->Ex.:	 AB' + AC + BC
		->Ans.: AB' + BC 
		
	->Ex.:	 (A+B) (A'+C) (B+C)
		->Ans.: (A+B) (A'+C)
		
	->Ex.:	 (A+B) (A+C) (B'+C)
		->Ans.: (A+B) (B'+C)
		
	->Ex.:	 (A'B') + (AC') + (B'C')
		->Ans.: A'B' + AC'
